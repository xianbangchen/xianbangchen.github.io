Xianbang Chen is a postdoctoral fellow at the Electric Power and Energy Systems Laboratory at Stevens Institute of Technology (SIT), collaborating with [Prof. Lei Wu](https://sites.google.com/site/leiwupes/about-me). He received his B.S. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering from Sichuan University (China) in 2017 and 2020, respectively, and completed his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Stevens Institute of Technology in 2024 under [Prof. Lei Wu](https://sites.google.com/site/leiwupes/about-me)â€™s supervision. His research focuses on enhancing the operational efficiency of modern power systems via the integration of hybrid optimization and learning methodologies.
